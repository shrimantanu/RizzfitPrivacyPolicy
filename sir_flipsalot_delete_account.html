Delete Your Account & Data

Sir Flipsalot — developed by Shaurya
Application ID: com.shaurya.sirflipsy
Last updated: 14 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Sir Flipsalot account and the data associated with it. Sir Flipsalot is fully playable without an account; an account exists only if you chose to sign in with Google to use the online leaderboard.

How to request deletion

  1. Open your email app and send a message from the same Google account email you used to sign in to the game (this lets us verify the request is really yours).
  2. Send it to shrimantanu@gmail.com.
  3. Use the subject line “Delete my data”.
  4. We will permanently delete your account record and confirm by reply. Deletion completes within 30 days of your request.
Email a deletion request

What data is deleted

When your request is processed, the following data is permanently deleted from our backend:

DataDescription
Account identifierYour Google account ID and the unique user ID we store for you
Email addressThe email associated with your sign-in
Display nameThe name shown next to your leaderboard score
Country & local areaRegion used to group your leaderboard ranking
Game progressYour best score, level reached, and in-game “bee” currency balance
Authentication recordYour sign-in entry in our authentication system

This removes your entry from the public leaderboard. Residual copies in our provider's encrypted backups are purged on the normal backup rotation, within the same 30-day window.

What data is kept

We do not retain any personal data about you after deletion completes. We keep no copy of your email, account identifier, name, or game progress. We may retain a minimal record of the deletion request itself (for example, that a request was fulfilled) only where required to comply with our legal obligations; this record does not identify you to other users.

Data stored only on your device

Some data — your sign-in tokens and your local best score, level, and bee balance — is stored only on your phone, never tied to your identity on our servers. You can remove it yourself at any time:

  • Sign out in the app to clear your stored sign-in tokens.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data from your device.
