Privacy Policy for Sir Flipsalot

Application ID: com.shaurya.sirflipsy
Last updated: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the mobile game Sir Flipsalot ("the App", "we", "us") collects, why we collect it, how it is used and stored, and the choices you have. By installing and using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Playing without an account

Sir Flipsalot is fully playable without signing in. If you never sign in, the App does not transmit any personal information about you to our servers. Your high score and in-game progress are stored only on your device.

2. Information we collect

We collect personal information only when you choose to sign in with Google to use the optional online leaderboard. We collect the minimum needed to identify your account, show your ranking, and save your progress across devices.

DataSourceWhy we collect it
Google account identifier and email addressGoogle Sign-In (you authorise this in your browser)To create and recognise your account on re-launch and across devices

We do not collect your precise (GPS) location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, SMS, call logs, or device advertising identifiers. The App contains no third-party advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not knowingly collect more data than is listed above.

3. Information that is publicly visible

If you sign in and submit a score, the following fields become part of a public leaderboard that any other player can view: your display name, country, local area (if provided), score, and level. Your email address and Google account identifier are never shown publicly. Please avoid putting personal information into your display name or area field, since those are public.

4. How information is stored

  • On your device: your sign-in tokens and your best score/level/bee balance are stored locally (in the app's saved preferences).
  • On our backend: the data in Section 2 is stored using Supabase, our hosting and database provider, in a data centre located in India (AWS ap-south-1, Mumbai). Access is protected by row-level security so that you can only modify your own record.

5. Third parties we share data with

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers that make the App work:

  • Google — provides the sign-in (authentication). Your use of Google Sign-In is also governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — provides our database and authentication backend (acting as our data processor). See the Supabase Privacy Policy.

6. Data retention and deletion

  • Sign out at any time from within the App. This removes your sign-in tokens from your device.
  • Delete your account and all associated data: email us at the address below from your account email, with the subject "Delete my data". We will permanently delete your account record (including your leaderboard entry and stored progress) within 30 days.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data from your device.

7. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

Data transmitted between the App and our backend is sent over encrypted HTTPS connections. Server-side access controls (row-level security) ensure each player can only modify their own record. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
