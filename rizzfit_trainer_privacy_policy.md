# Privacy Policy — Rizzfit Trainer

**Effective date:** 18 June 2026

Rizzfit ("we", "us", "our") operates the **Rizzfit Trainer** Android application (the "App").
This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you
have. By using the App you agree to this policy.

---

## 1. Who we are

Rizzfit Trainer is the app for gym trainers and coaches. It lets you sign in, manage the clients
assigned to you, build and assign workout and diet plans, view clients' check-ins and the body and
health information they share with you, pair with new clients by scanning a QR code, and chat
privately with your clients and your gym. The App connects to our backend hosted on **Supabase**.

**Contact:** support@rizzfit.in

---

## 2. Information we collect

We only collect what the App needs to function. We do **not** sell your data and we do **not**
use it for advertising.

### a) Information you provide
- **Account & profile:** your **name**, **email address**, and **phone number**.
- **Trainer profile:** your **bio, specializations, certifications, and experience**.
- **Plans:** the **workout and diet plans** you create for your clients.
- **Messages:** the content of **in-app chat messages** you exchange with your clients and gym.

### b) Information about your clients
As a trainer, you view and manage personal data about the **clients assigned to you** — including
their names, the body and health information they choose to share, the workout and diet plans you
create for them, their check-ins, and your chat messages. We process this on behalf of you and your
gym so you can coach your clients. You should access and use client data **only for legitimate
coaching purposes**. Your clients manage their own accounts through the separate Rizzfit Member app.

### c) Information created as you use the App
- **App activity:** basic interaction data needed to operate features.
- **Identifiers:** a **user ID** that links your account to your data.
- **Push token:** a **device push token** issued by Google Firebase Cloud Messaging, stored on
  our backend so we can send you notifications. You can turn notifications off at any time.

### d) Information from Google Sign-In
When you sign in with Google, Google shares your **name, email address, and a unique account
identifier** with the App so we can create and secure your account. We do not receive your Google
password.

### e) Device permissions
The App uses the following Android permissions:

- **Internet** — to communicate with our backend.
- **Notifications** (Android 13+) — to show push notifications about your clients, messages, and
  gym updates. You can turn these off at any time in the App's Settings or device settings.
- **Camera** (optional) — used **only** to scan a client's QR code when you pair with them. Camera
  frames are processed **on your device** to read the code; we do **not** capture photos or video,
  and we do **not** upload or store any camera images. The App still installs and works on devices
  without a camera.

The App does **not** access your microphone, contacts, precise location, or device advertising ID.

---

## 3. How we use your information

We use your information to:
- create and secure your account and sign you in;
- let you manage the clients assigned to you;
- build and assign workout and diet plans, and view clients' check-ins and shared health information;
- pair you with a new client when you scan their QR code;
- deliver private chat between you and your clients and gym;
- send you push notifications about your clients and messages (you can opt out at any time);
- operate, maintain, and improve the App;
- respond to support requests.

---

## 4. How your information is shared

We do **not** sell your personal data or share it with third parties for their own marketing.
We share data only with service providers that operate the App on our behalf:

- **Supabase** — our backend and database host, which stores your account, your trainer profile,
  messages, the plans you create, and the client data you manage so the App can function.
- **Google** — provides sign-in (Google Identity) and app distribution (Google Play).
- **Google Firebase Cloud Messaging** — delivers push notifications to your device. We share your
  device's push token with this service so notifications can reach you.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

---

## 5. Data security

Data is encrypted **in transit** using HTTPS/TLS between the App and our backend. Access to your
data is restricted by row-level security so that you can only see your own information and the
clients you are authorised to coach. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we
take reasonable measures to protect your data.

---

## 6. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App.
When you ask us to delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where
we must keep it to comply with legal obligations.

---

## 7. Your rights and choices

You can:
- **Access or correct** your profile information from within the App.
- **Request deletion** of your account and associated personal data by emailing us at
  **support@rizzfit.in** — see our [account deletion page](https://shrimantanu.github.io/RizzfitPrivacyPolicy/rizzfit_trainer_delete_account.html)
  for the full steps. We will action your request within a reasonable time.
- **Withdraw consent** by discontinuing use of the App and requesting deletion.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or
India's data protection laws. Contact us to exercise them.

---

## 8. Children's privacy

The App is intended for users **18 years and older** and is not directed at children. We do not
knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data,
contact us and we will delete it.

may be located outside your country. By using the App you consent to such processing.
