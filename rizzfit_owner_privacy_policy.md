# Privacy Policy — Rizzfit Owner

**Effective date:** 18 June 2026

Rizzfit ("we", "us", "our") operates the **Rizzfit Owner** Android application (the "App").
This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you
have. By using the App you agree to this policy.

---

## 1. Who we are

Rizzfit Owner is the management app for gym owners and operators. It lets you sign in and run
your gym — manage members and their memberships, record and collect dues, scan a member's QR
code to mark attendance, manage your trainers and team, track leads, send announcements, and
chat with your members. The App connects to our backend hosted on **Supabase**.

**Contact:** support@rizzfit.in

---

## 2. Information we collect

We only collect what the App needs to function. We do **not** sell your data and we do **not**
use it for advertising.

### a) Information you provide
- **Account & profile:** your **name**, **email address**, and **phone number**.
- **Gym profile:** your **gym's name, address, and location pin**.
- **Messages:** the content of **in-app chat messages** you exchange with your members.

### b) Information you manage about other people
As the gym operator, you enter and manage personal data about your **members, trainers, and
leads** (such as their names, contact details, memberships, dues, and attendance). We process
this on your behalf so you can run your gym. **You are responsible for having a lawful basis to
provide it and for informing those individuals as required by applicable law.** Your members
manage their own accounts through the separate Rizzfit Member app.

### c) Information created as you use the App
- **Gym operations:** payment and dues records, attendance you record, and other gym activity.
- **App activity:** basic interaction data needed to operate features.
- **Identifiers:** a **user ID** that links your account to your data.
- **Push token:** a **device push token** issued by Google Firebase Cloud Messaging, stored on
  our backend so we can send you notifications. You can turn notifications off at any time.

### d) Information from Google Sign-In
When you sign in with Google, Google shares your **name, email address, and a unique account
identifier** with the App so we can create and secure your account. We do not receive your
Google password.

### e) Device permissions
The App uses the following Android permissions:

- **Internet** — to communicate with our backend.
- **Notifications** (Android 13+) — to show push notifications about member activity, messages,
  and gym updates. You can turn these off at any time in the App's Settings or device settings.
- **Camera** (optional) — used **only** to scan a member's QR code to mark their attendance.
  Camera frames are processed **on your device** to read the code; we do **not** capture photos
  or video, and we do **not** upload or store any camera images. The App still installs and
  works on devices without a camera.
- **Location** (optional, approximate or precise) — used **only** when you tap **"Use my current
  location"** while setting up or editing your gym's profile, to drop a single map pin for your
  gym. We read your location **once, in the foreground**, at that moment only. We do **not**
  track your location in the background, and we do **not** use it for advertising. You can skip
  this and type your gym's address instead.

The App does **not** access your microphone, contacts, or device advertising ID.

---

## 3. How we use your information

We use your information to:
- create and secure your account and sign you in;
- let you manage members, memberships, dues, attendance, trainers, and leads for your gym;
- mark member attendance when you scan their QR code;
- deliver chat between you and your members, and send announcements;
- save your gym's profile and location so members can find it;
- send you push notifications about member activity and messages (you can opt out at any time);
- operate, maintain, and improve the App;
- respond to support requests.

---

## 4. How your information is shared

We do **not** sell your personal data or share it with third parties for their own marketing.
We share data only with service providers that operate the App on our behalf:

- **Supabase** — our backend and database host, which stores your account, your gym's records,
  messages, and the member, trainer, and lead data you manage so the App can function.
- **Google** — provides sign-in (Google Identity) and app distribution (Google Play). Dues and
  payment records you enter are stored to track memberships; Rizzfit does **not** process card
  payments and does **not** store payment card details.
- **Google Firebase Cloud Messaging** — delivers push notifications to your device. We share
  your device's push token with this service so notifications can reach you.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

---

## 5. Data security

Data is encrypted **in transit** using HTTPS/TLS between the App and our backend. Access to your
data is restricted by row-level security so that you can only see your own gym's information and
the people you are authorised to manage. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but
we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

---

## 6. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App.
When you ask us to delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where
we must keep it to comply with legal obligations.

---

## 7. Your rights and choices

You can:
- **Access or correct** your profile and gym information from within the App.
- **Request deletion** of your account and associated personal data by emailing us at
  **support@rizzfit.in** — see our [account deletion page](https://shrimantanu.github.io/RizzfitPrivacyPolicy/rizzfit_owner_delete_account.html)
  for the full steps. We will action your request within a reasonable time.
- **Withdraw consent** by discontinuing use of the App and requesting deletion.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or
India's data protection laws. Contact us to exercise them.

---

## 8. Children's privacy

The App is intended for users **18 years and older** and is not directed at children. We do not
knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data,
contact us and we will delete it.
