Rizzfit ("we", "us", "our") operates the **Rizzfit Member** Android application (the "App").
This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you
have. By using the App you agree to this policy.

---

## 1. Who we are

Rizzfit Member is a companion app for gym members. It lets you sign in, view your membership,
record gym check-ins, follow a diet plan, and chat privately with your trainer or gym owner.
The App connects to our backend hosted on **Supabase**.

**Contact:** shrimantanu@gmail.com

---

## 2. Information we collect

We only collect what the App needs to function. We do **not** sell your data and we do **not**
use it for advertising.

### a) Information you provide
- **Account & profile:** your **name**, **email address**, and **phone number**.
- **Messages:** the content of **in-app chat messages** you exchange with your trainer or gym owner.

### b) Information created as you use the App
- **Fitness activity:** your **gym check-ins** and **diet plan** information.
- **App activity:** basic interaction data needed to operate features (e.g. which screens/actions
  you use).
- **Identifiers:** a **user ID** that links your account to your data.

### c) Information from Google Sign-In
When you sign in with Google, Google shares your **name, email address, and a unique account
identifier** with the App so we can create and secure your account. We do not receive your Google
password.

We request only the **Internet** permission. The App does **not** access your camera, microphone,
contacts, precise location, or device advertising ID.

---

## 3. How we use your information

We use your information to:
- create and secure your account and sign you in;
- show your membership, check-ins, and diet plan;
- deliver private chat between you and your trainer or gym owner;
- operate, maintain, and improve the App;
- respond to support requests.

---

## 4. How your information is shared

We do **not** sell your personal data or share it with third parties for their own marketing.
We share data only with service providers that operate the App on our behalf:

- **Supabase** — our backend and database host, which stores your account data, messages,
  check-ins, and diet plan so the App can function.
- **Google** — provides sign-in (Google Identity) and app distribution (Google Play). If you
  purchase a subscription in the future, payments are processed by **Google Play**; we never
  receive or store your card or payment details.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

---

## 5. Data security

Data is encrypted **in transit** using HTTPS/TLS between the App and our backend. Access to your
data is restricted by row-level security so that you can only see your own information and the
people you are authorised to communicate with. No method of transmission or storage is 100%
secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

---

## 6. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App.
When you ask us to delete your account, we delete your personal data within a reasonable period,
except where we must keep it to comply with legal obligations.

---

## 7. Your rights and choices

You can:
- **Access or correct** your profile information from within the App.
- **Request deletion** of your account and associated personal data by emailing us at
  **shrimantanu@gmail.com**. We will action your request within a reasonable time.
- **Withdraw consent** by discontinuing use of the App and requesting deletion.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or
India's data protection laws. Contact us to exercise them.

---

## 8. Children's privacy

The App is intended for users **18 years and older** and is not directed at children. We do not
knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data,
contact us and we will delete it.

---

## 9. International users

Your information may be processed and stored on servers operated by our service providers, which
may be located outside your country. By using the App you consent to such processing.
