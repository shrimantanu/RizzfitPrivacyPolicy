Privacy Policy — Rizzfit Trainer

Application ID: com.rizzfit.trainer
Effective date: 18 June 2026

Rizzfit ("we", "us", "our") operates the Rizzfit Trainer Android application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Rizzfit Trainer is the app for gym trainers and coaches. It lets you sign in, manage the clients assigned to you, build and assign workout and diet plans, view clients' check-ins and the body and health information they share with you, pair with new clients by scanning a QR code, and chat privately with your clients and your gym. The App connects to our backend hosted on Supabase.

Contact: support@rizzfit.in

We only collect what the App needs to function. We do not sell your data and we do not use it for advertising.

When you sign in with Google, Google shares your name, email address, and a unique account identifier with the App so we can create and secure your account. We do not receive your Google password.

Data about your clients. As a trainer, you view and manage personal data about the clients assigned to you — including their names, the body and health information they choose to share, the workout and diet plans you create for them, their check-ins, and your chat messages. We process this information on behalf of you and your gym so you can coach your clients. You should access and use client data only for legitimate coaching purposes. Your clients manage their own accounts through the separate Rizzfit Member app, which is governed by its own privacy policy.

3. Device permissions

The App uses the following Android permissions:

  • Internet — to communicate with our backend.
  • Notifications (Android 13+) — to show push notifications about your clients, messages, and gym updates. You can turn these off at any time in the App's Settings or in your device settings.
  • Camera (optional) — used only to scan a client's QR code when you pair with them. Camera frames are processed on your device to read the code; we do not capture photos or video, and we do not upload or store any camera images. The App still installs and works on devices without a camera.

The App does not access your microphone, contacts, precise location, or device advertising ID.

4. How we use your information

  • create and secure your account and sign you in;
  • let you manage the clients assigned to you;
  • build and assign workout and diet plans, and view clients' check-ins and shared health information;
  • pair you with a new client when you scan their QR code;
  • deliver private chat between you and your clients and gym;
  • send you push notifications about your clients and messages (you can opt out at any time);
  • operate, maintain, and improve the App;
  • respond to support requests.

5. How your information is shared

We do not sell your personal data or share it with third parties for their own marketing. We share data only with service providers that operate the App on our behalf:

  • Supabase — our backend and database host, which stores your account, your trainer profile, messages, the plans you create, and the client data you manage so the App can function. See the Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google — provides sign-in (Google Identity) and app distribution (Google Play). See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — delivers push notifications to your device. We share your device's push token with this service so notifications can reach you.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

6. Data security

Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS between the App and our backend. Access to your data is restricted by row-level security so that you can only see your own information and the clients you are authorised to coach. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

7. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. When you ask us to delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we must keep it to comply with legal obligations.

8. Your rights and choices

  • Access or correct your profile information from within the App.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data by emailing us at support@rizzfit.in — see our account deletion page for the full steps. We will action your request within a reasonable time.
  • Withdraw consent by discontinuing use of the App and requesting deletion.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or India's data protection laws. Contact us to exercise them.

9. Children's privacy

The App is intended for users 18 years and older and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International users

Your information may be processed and stored on servers operated by our service providers, which may be located outside your country. By using the App you consent to such processing.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new "Effective date" on this page. Material changes will be communicated through the App or by email where appropriate.
