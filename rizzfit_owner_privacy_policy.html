Privacy Policy — Rizzfit Owner

Application ID: com.rizzfit.owner
Effective date: 18 June 2026

Rizzfit ("we", "us", "our") operates the Rizzfit Owner Android application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Rizzfit Owner is the management app for gym owners and operators. It lets you sign in and run your gym — manage members and their memberships, record and collect dues, scan a member's QR code to mark attendance, manage your trainers and team, track leads, send announcements, and chat with your members. The App connects to our backend hosted on Supabase.

Contact: support@rizzfit.in

We only collect what the App needs to function. We do not sell your data and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Device permissions

The App uses the following Android permissions:

  • Internet — to communicate with our backend.
  • Notifications (Android 13+) — to show push notifications about member activity, messages, and gym updates. You can turn these off at any time in the App's Settings or in your device settings.
  • Camera (optional) — used only to scan a member's QR code to mark their attendance. Camera frames are processed on your device to read the code; we do not capture photos or video, and we do not upload or store any camera images. The App still installs and works on devices without a camera.
  • Location (optional, approximate or precise) — used only when you tap "Use my current location" while setting up or editing your gym's profile, to drop a single map pin for your gym. We read your location once, in the foreground, at that moment only. We do not track your location in the background, and we do not use it for advertising. You can skip this and type your gym's address instead.

The App does not access your microphone, contacts, or device advertising ID.

4. How we use your information

  • create and secure your account and sign you in;
  • let you manage members, memberships, dues, attendance, trainers, and leads for your gym;
  • mark member attendance when you scan their QR code;
  • deliver chat between you and your members, and send announcements;
  • save your gym's profile and location so members can find it;
  • send you push notifications about member activity and messages (you can opt out at any time);
  • operate, maintain, and improve the App;
  • respond to support requests.

5. How your information is shared

We do not sell your personal data or share it with third parties for their own marketing. We share data only with service providers that operate the App on our behalf:

  • Supabase — our backend and database host, which stores your account, your gym's records, messages, and the member, trainer, and lead data you manage so the App can function. See the Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google — provides sign-in (Google Identity) and app distribution (Google Play). See the Google Privacy Policy. Dues and payment records you enter are stored to track memberships; Rizzfit does not process card payments and does not store payment card details.
  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — delivers push notifications to your device. We share your device's push token with this service so notifications can reach you.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

6. Data security

Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS between the App and our backend. Access to your data is restricted by row-level security so that you can only see your own gym's information and the people you are authorised to manage. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

7. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. When you ask us to delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we must keep it to comply with legal obligations.

8. Your rights and choices

  • Access or correct your profile and gym information from within the App.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data by emailing us at support@rizzfit.in — see our account deletion page for the full steps. We will action your request within a reasonable time.
  • Withdraw consent by discontinuing use of the App and requesting deletion.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or India's data protection laws. Contact us to exercise them.

9. Children's privacy

The App is intended for users 18 years and older and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International users

Your information may be processed and stored on servers operated by our service providers, which may be located outside your country. By using the App you consent to such processing.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new "Effective date" on this page. Material changes will be communicated through the App or by email where appropriate.
