Delete Your Account & Data

Rizzfit Owner — developed by Rizzfit
Application ID: com.rizzfit.owner
Last updated: 18 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Rizzfit Owner account and the data associated with it. Rizzfit Owner uses an account that is created when you sign in (with Google or your email) to manage your gym — its members, dues, attendance, team, and chats.

How to request deletion

  1. Open your email app and send a message from the same email address associated with your Rizzfit Owner account (this lets us verify the request is really yours).
  2. Send it to support@rizzfit.in.
  3. Use the subject line “Delete my account”.
  4. We will permanently delete your account and associated data and confirm by reply. Deletion completes within 30 days of your request.
Email a deletion request

What data is deleted

When your request is processed, the following data is permanently deleted from our backend:

DataDescription
Account & profileYour name, email address, and phone number
User IDThe unique identifier that links your account to your data
In-app messagesThe chat messages you exchanged with your members
Push tokenYour device's Firebase Cloud Messaging notification token
Authentication recordYour sign-in entry in our authentication system

What about my gym's data?

If you operate a gym, that gym also has records you created and manage — your members, trainers, leads, dues, and attendance. Because these can affect other people (for example, your members who use the Rizzfit Member app), please tell us in your email whether you also want the gym and its records removed. Before we proceed we will confirm the options with you — for example deleting the gym and its records, or transferring ownership — so nothing is lost unexpectedly. Your members can independently manage or delete their own accounts through the Rizzfit Member app.

What data is kept

We do not retain personal data about you after deletion completes. We keep no copy of your name, email, phone number, or messages. Residual copies in our provider's (Supabase) encrypted backups are purged on the normal backup rotation, within the same 30-day window. We may retain a minimal record of the deletion request itself (for example, that a request was fulfilled) only where required to comply with our legal obligations; this record does not identify you to other users.

Data stored only on your device

Your sign-in session token is stored only on your phone and is never tied to your identity on our servers. You can remove it yourself at any time:

  • Sign out in the app to clear your stored sign-in session.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data from your device.
